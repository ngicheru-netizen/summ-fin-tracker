This website is a Student Finance Tracker that helps students manage their finances effectively. It allows users to track their income and expenses, set budgets, and monitor their financial health over time. Currently, the Finance Tracker allows the students to monitor their spending in 3 currencies: Kenyan Shillings, U.S Dollar and Rwandan Francs.
